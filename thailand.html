Day 1 : Auli

After you reach the Delhi International Airport, get an assisted transfer to your pre-booked hotel. The rest of the day is free for leisure activities like interacting with the locals, relishing the street food, exploring the nearby surroundings on foot. Stay Overnight.

Day 2 : Nainital

Morning after breakfast, proceed for city tour covering the attractions of Old and New Delhi. The list of the tourist attractions includes Jama Masjid, Red Fort, Gandhi Memorial, India Gate, Parliament House, Humayun’s Tomb and the Complex of Qutab Minar. Night stay at the hotel.

Day 3 : Mussoorie

Post breakfast on the third day; get transported by road to Agra. On arrival, check-in your hotel. Have lunch and leave for city tour of Agra. Today, you visit the Agra Fort – a beautiful Mughal architectural masterpiece and the Tomb of Itmad-ud-Daulah- commissioned by Empress Noor Jehan in memory of her father. The evening is for visiting the extremely famous Taj Mahal- one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was erected by Mughal Emperor Shah Jehan in the memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4 : Kausani

Today, after breakfast, begin driving towards the Ranthambore National Park, one of the largest national parks in the northern region of the country. On the way, stop to explore the old deserted Mughal city of Fatehpur Sikri- a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Once inside, visit its major structural elements such as Salim Chisti's Tomb, Jama Masjid, Panch Mahal and Jodha Bai’s Palace. The next visit is the Keoladeo Ghana National Park in Bharatpur, formerly known as Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary. Enjoy bird watching where you can spot as many as 366 avifauna species including Painted Stork, Great Egret, Common Parakeet etc. Proceed towards Ranthambore. On arrival, check- in your pre-booked hotel for an overnight stay.
