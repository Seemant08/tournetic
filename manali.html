Day 01 :Delhi – Manali (By Volvo Bus)

On arrival at Delhi by afternoon, come to the Boarding Point at 5:00 PM. Bus start the 5:30 PM. overnight journey in Volvo bus for Delhi to Manali by road.

Day 02 : Manali Sightseeing

Morning on arrival at Manali you will be pickup by our representative and transfer to your Prebooked hotel. Check in to hotel. Afternoon half day city tour visiting Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Vashishth Temple, Club House and Tibetan Monastery. evening free to visit mall road. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 03 : Manali – Rohtang Pass / Snow Point – Manali

After Breakfast at hotel. Today full day excursion to snow Point visiting Kothi Village, Gulaba, Marhi, Sollang valley and Rohtang Pass (Tourist Spot in Himalaya having Snow all 12 months). Rohtang Pass situated at an altitude of 3979 mtrs above sea level and 51 kms outside Manali is the mighty Rohtang Pass - The Gateway to Lahaul-Spiti Valley. Whenever Rohtang Pass is closed due to bad weather conditions, people will be allowed to visit up to Snow Point or Solang Valley. Evening drive back to hotel. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 04 : Manali – Kullu & Manikaran – Manali

Morning after Breakfast full day excursion to Kullu Valley, Kasol Valley, Angora Rabbit Farm House, vaishno Temple, River Rafting and Manikaran (the holy hot Sulpher water spring) full day sightseeing. Evening drive back to hotel. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 05 : Manali – Delhi (By Volvo Bus)

Morning after Beakfast take some rest at hotel. Enjoy shopping at Mall Road then check out from the hotel. Evening at 4:00 PM catch Volvo bus from private bus stand. Overnight journey in Volvo bus.
